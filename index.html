The Problem

Clinics were not built for the chaos they face today.

Every morning, dozens of patients crowd into waiting rooms with no clear order, no time estimates, and no way to plan their day. Doctors fall behind. Patients grow frustrated. Staff manage chaos instead of care.

Overbooking, no-shows, paper records, and phone-only scheduling create a system that fails everyone involved particularly in cities where demand far outpaces clinic capacity.

No time estimates for patients
Manual, phone-only booking
Overcrowded waiting rooms
No-show slots go to waste
The Solution

Medma brings order and calm back to local clinics.

Medma is a smart clinic management platform built specifically for local healthcare in Morocco. Patients book remotely, receive real-time queue updates, and arrive only when their turn is near.

  • Smart Queue Management

    Dynamic scheduling that adapts to real-world clinic pace, reducing idle time and overflow.

  • Remote Follow-ups

    Patients receive updates and reminders via SMS, cutting no-shows and walk-in overflow.

  • Clinic Dashboard

    Doctors and staff gain a real-time overview of the day's flow, bookings, and patient status.

  • Zero Setup Friction

    Clinics go live in minutes. No hardware, no training overhead, no disruption to daily operations.

Our Mission
We are on a mission to bring calm to local clinics and give patients their time back.

Healthcare should feel like care not like waiting in line. We believe every patient deserves to know when they will be seen, and every doctor deserves a practice that runs with clarity.
